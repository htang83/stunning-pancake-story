The main character of the story Magic Pen Ma Liang is Ma Liang. In the story, he received a magic pen from God. Anything he drew with the pen be to lifes.

Ma Liang wanted a friend so he drew a monkey that he could keep as a pet. Ma Liang loved his monkey, but he wanted to make more things because he wasn't satisfied.

Ma Liang drew a table and a stick, and he found out that the monkey started to play around with the table and stick. So he realized that monkey can be as smart as a human being.
